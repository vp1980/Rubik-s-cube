🧩 Rubik's Cube — Anatomy Reference

Quick reference sheet • Lesson 1

⭐ Golden Rule: Center pieces NEVER move. They define the colour of each face.

The 3 Piece Types

Type Count Colours Location
Centers ⭐ 6 1 colour Middle of each face. Never move.
Edges 🟣 12 2 colours Between two centers. On every edge of the cube.
Corners 🔴 8 3 colours At every corner of the cube.

Standard Face Colours (most cubes)

White — usually the top/bottom
Yellow — opposite to White
Red — opposite to Orange
Orange — opposite to Red
Blue — opposite to Green
Green — opposite to Blue
