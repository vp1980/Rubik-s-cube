# Learner is an 8-year-old starting from zero

The learner is 8 years old with no prior knowledge of the Rubik's Cube — not the piece types, not the notation, not any algorithms. This sets the floor for the zone of proximal development.

**Implications:** Begin with cube anatomy (Lesson 1: centers, edges, corners) before introducing any moves or algorithms. All language must be child-appropriate. One concept per lesson. Never introduce an algorithm before the learner understands what piece it is solving and why.
